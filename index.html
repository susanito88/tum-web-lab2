About Molda

Welcome to Molda, where traditional Moldovan hospitality meets exceptional event planning. We specialize in hosting memorable celebrations - from intimate birthdays to grand weddings and corporate gatherings.

Our spacious venue accommodates 20-100 guests, and our experienced team ensures every detail of your event is perfect. We bring the authentic flavors of Moldova to your celebration with traditional dishes prepared by our master chefs.
